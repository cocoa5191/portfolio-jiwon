Emoji Proposal
2023 Web, AI
This page is an archive of published emojis and rejected emoji proposals that were submitted to the emoji Consortium from 2020 to 2023 .

As for why a Fly in the year 2020 was more appropriate than Biscuit, I have no Idea, but if you're curious, you can send an email to the Emoji Consortium.

This website is created to honor emoji that may or may not be in use in parallel universes, to convey information about proposals that are deemed appropriate or inappropriate to someone, to inspire someone who might submit a new emoji proposal after viewing this archive, or for those who have a deep love for emojis.
